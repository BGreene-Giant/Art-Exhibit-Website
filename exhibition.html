Ava Smith's Art Work


Winter Wonders

This was the first artwork Smith every created, she gained the inspiration when viewing a winter morning through a waterdroplet on her window.


Latern-Lit Town

Smith used her love for Disney and Chinese culture to create a exquisite watercolered city cebrating the festival!


Rainy Days

While growing up, Smith loved rainy days; however, all she could remeber from those nostalgic memories is the vibrant clothing of individuals cowering under umbrellas.


Natures Call

Smith's works toward the middle of her career focused on nature, in addition, Smith viewd the wolf as the centerpeice of all nature depictions, thus creating this masterpeice.


Birds of Many Feathers

Smith has allways loved birds and made many works involve them; however, the one peice she is really proud of depicts many differnt types of birds under a vibrate tree.


Natures Retreat

Smith loved to escape the confines of the city and retreat to her cabin she owned in the forest. This painting shows that very cabin.


Angelic Demon

Near the end of Smith's career, she became infatuated with angels and demons aswell as life and death. This artwork depicts what Smith classified as an "Angelic Demon".


Earth's Core

This artwork depicts what Smith believes the the underworld, located deep within the Earth's core, may look like.


Deadly Flourish

This was Smith's last peice, she noted that "All goods things must come to an end" and "Everything that is beautiful will eventually fade away" when she finished the peice.
